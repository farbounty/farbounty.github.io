Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 29 2025

Transparency Note: Bounty is built with privacy and decentralization in mind. We collect minimal information and leverage blockchain technology for transparency.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Wallet Information

  • Public wallet addresses for transactions
  • Transaction history on the platform
  • No private keys or seed phrases

1.2 Platform Usage Data

  • Bounty creation and completion history
  • User ratings and reviews
  • Communication within the platform
  • IP addresses for security purposes

1.3 Farcaster Integration

  • Farcaster user ID (if connected)
  • Public profile information
  • No private messages or data

2. How We Use Information

2.1 Platform Operation

  • Facilitate bounty payments and escrow
  • Provide dispute resolution services
  • Maintain user reputation systems
  • Ensure platform security

2.2 Communication

  • Notify users of bounty updates
  • Send platform announcements
  • Provide transaction confirmations

2.3 Analytics and Improvement

  • Understand platform usage patterns
  • Improve user experience
  • Identify and fix technical issues

3. Information Sharing

3.1 What We Share

  • Bounty details with potential hunters
  • User reputation scores
  • Successful transaction information
  • Aggregate, anonymized platform statistics

3.2 What We Don't Share

  • Personal identifying information
  • Private wallet data
  • Individual transaction amounts (unless public by nature)
  • Private communications

4. Blockchain Transparency

4.1 All bounty payments and platform transactions are recorded on the blockchain, which is inherently public.

4.2 We cannot modify or delete blockchain transactions once confirmed.

4.3 Users should consider the public nature of blockchain when transacting.

5. Data Security

5.1 Protection Measures

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security audits
  • Minimal data retention

5.2 User Responsibility

  • Keep wallet credentials secure
  • Use strong authentication methods
  • Verify transaction details before confirming

6. Data Retention

6.1 We retain platform data only as long as necessary for operation.

6.2 Users can request deletion of non-essential personal data.

6.3 Blockchain data is permanent and cannot be deleted.

7. Donation Information

7.1 Donation transactions are recorded on the blockchain and are publicly visible.

7.2 We may publicly acknowledge large donors (with permission).

7.3 Donation usage is transparently reported as outlined in our funding goals.

8. Third-Party Services

8.1 Farcaster Integration

  • We integrate with Farcaster for user authentication
  • Farcaster's privacy policy applies to their services
  • We only access public Farcaster profile information

8.2 Blockchain Networks

  • We interact with various blockchain networks
  • Blockchain data is public and immutable
  • Network-specific privacy considerations apply

9. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access their personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of non-essential data
  • Export their platform data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

10. Children's Privacy

Bounty is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

11. International Users

11.1 Bounty operates globally on decentralized infrastructure.

11.2 Users are responsible for complying with local laws regarding cryptocurrency and online platforms.

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We will notify users of significant changes to this privacy policy through platform announcements.

13. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or data requests, please contact us through the Bounty platform.

Remember: Bounty empowers builders and contributors through transparent, community-driven development. Your privacy and trust are essential to our mission.
